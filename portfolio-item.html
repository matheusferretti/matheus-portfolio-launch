SYMP-WEB

A Freelancing App

This project was developed envisioning a better future for developers and employers. The increase in COVID-19 cases facilitated the extension of remote job opportunities all across the world, and with that comes more flexibility for developers. This platform is here to ease the transition to that flexibility.

SYMP-WEB is mainly a React.js web app, but the design came out to be pleasing due to the application of amazing Bootstrap features. Even though SYMP-WEB is solely a front-end project, I was able to make it dynamic enough to support information provided by different mock users for the sake of testing user-specificity.

Creating this project, my intention was to convey to the user the different privileges entailed between creating accounts as "Freelancers" and/or "Employers".
